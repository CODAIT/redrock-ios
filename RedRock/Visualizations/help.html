RedRock allows users to:

  • Search for trends on Twitter
  • Leverage the power of Spark, that was previously only available to data scientists, to do big data analysis
  • Share findings via Email
  • Respond directly to Tweeters from within the app
  • Provide feedback directly to the developers from within the app
